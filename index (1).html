We Know What You're Thinking
Every concern. Addressed.
"Has this been independently validated?"
Bench-scale testing confirms 5-6V cell voltage, 50A capacity, pH 0 membrane survival. Seed funds will pay for third-party metallurgical lab validation (SGS, ALS) within 90 days of close.
"Can it scale beyond bench?"
The electrochemistry is inherently modular — add cells in parallel. No scale-up chemistry risk. The pilot is literally N copies of the bench unit in a container.
"What about membrane longevity?"
At <$5/m², membranes are a consumable, not a capital item. Even with monthly replacement, cost is negligible vs. Nafion. Field-replaceable in minutes.
"Single founder risk?"
IP is patented and documented. Open-source membrane recipe on GitHub ensures knowledge isn't locked in one person. Seed funds specifically allocated to hire key team members.
"Why hasn't a big company done this?"
They tried — with $500/m² Nafion membranes. The math never worked. Our membrane is the missing piece. The science was never the problem. The economics were.
"What's the IP moat?"
Utility patent #19/531,984 filed. The membrane recipe is open-source (strategic — grows the ecosystem), but the system integration, process control, and mining-specific application are proprietary.
