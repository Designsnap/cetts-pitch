Process Flow
What goes in. What comes out.
Closed-loop system. Acids regenerated. Zero discharge.

INPUT

  • Mine tailings slurry
  • HCl + NaClO₃ (leach agent)
  • Electricity (5–6V DC)
  • Water (makeup only)

CETTS PROCESS

  • Electrochemical dissolution
  • Selective ion migration
  • Membrane separation
  • Electrodeposition
  • Acid regeneration loop

OUTPUT

  • Pure metal cathodes (Cu, Au, Ni…)
  • Clean water (reusable)
  • Inert mineral residue
  • Regenerated acids (recycled)
  • Zero liquid discharge
We Know What You're Thinking
Every hard question. Answered.
We've heard these concerns. Here's how we address them.
"Has this been independently validated?"
Bench-scale testing confirms core electrochemistry. Seed funding enables third-party validation at SGS or ALS within 9 months. We welcome skepticism — that's why we're raising.
"Can a DIY membrane work at industrial scale?"
The membrane uses water softener resin + PVC cement + fiberglass — all industrial-grade materials. The recipe is open-source and reproducible. Scale-up is about cell stack engineering, not membrane chemistry.
"What about membrane longevity?"
Current membranes cost <$5/m² and are field-replaceable in minutes. Even if lifespan is 6 months, replacement cost is negligible vs. Nafion at $500–800/m². Economics work even with frequent replacement.
"This is a one-person operation."
Today, yes. That's the point of this raise — to build the team. The science is proven. What's needed is engineering scale-up, which is a solved problem in electrochemistry. We're hiring metallurgists and process engineers.
"What's the IP position?"
Non-provisional utility patent filed (App #19/531,984). The membrane recipe is open-source by design — our moat is the integrated system design, process control IP, and first-mover deployment data.
"Why open-source the membrane?"
Inspired by the RepRap model. Open-sourcing the membrane grows the ecosystem and proves the technology faster. Our value is in the complete system — not a single component. Open membrane = faster adoption = bigger market for CETTS.
