Section 02

The Problem

A $50 billion environmental liability that's also a massive untapped resource.
⚠️

Acid Mine Drainage

Sulfide minerals in tailings oxidize, generating sulfuric acid that leaches heavy metals into waterways. Affects 20,000+ sites globally.

💰

Stranded Value

Billions in recoverable metals (Cu, Au, Li, REEs) locked in tailings dams. Current extraction methods are too expensive or environmentally destructive.

📋

Regulatory Pressure

Tightening EPA, EU, and APAC regulations mandate active tailings treatment. Non-compliance penalties exceed $50K/day in many jurisdictions.

🏭

Legacy Methods Fail

Lime neutralization creates secondary sludge. SX-EW requires large footprints. Bioleaching is slow. None recover metals economically at small scale.

Section 03

The CETTS Solution

Electrochemistry meets modular engineering for a closed-loop treatment system.
Core Innovation

Open-Source Ion Exchange Membrane

Proprietary membrane made from water softener resin, PVC cement, and fiberglass mesh. Cost: $15–30 per unit vs. $500+ for Nafion. Acid-resistant, high ion selectivity, field-replaceable.

Architecture

Containerized Modular Units

Each CETTS unit fits in a standard 20ft shipping container. Deploy to any mine site in weeks. Stack units for throughput scaling. No civil works required.

Process

Selective Electrochemical Recovery

Continuous dissolution using regenerated HCl + sodium chlorate at room temperature. Selective plating of target metals. Acid recycled in closed loop.

Environmental

Zero Waste Stream

All acids regenerated. Process water recycled. Residual solids are inert calcium/silica compounds suitable for backfill. Zero liquid discharge capable.

Section 04

How It Works

Three-stage continuous process from raw tailings to refined metals.
Stage 01

Leach & Dissolve

Tailings slurry enters the Continuous Mining Unit (CMU). HCl + sodium chlorate dissolves target metals at room temperature. Acid is continuously regenerated.

→
Stage 02

Separate & Purify

Pregnant solution passes through ion exchange membranes. Selective separation of metal ions by charge and size. Multi-stage electrodialysis for high purity.

→
Stage 03

Plate & Recover

Continuous Refining Unit (CRU) electroplates pure metals onto cathodes. 99.5%+ purity achievable. Cathodes harvested on scheduled cycles.
